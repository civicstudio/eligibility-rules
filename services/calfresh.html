Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible, an applicant must meet all of the following requirements:

  1. 1. Gross Income
    household_income_fpl <= 200 percent

    Gross monthly income must be at or below 200% of the Federal Poverty Level (California uses Broad-Based Categorical Eligibility).

    Source
  2. 2. California Residency
    state_residency = CA

    Must be a California resident.

  3. 3. Citizenship/Immigration
    citizenship_status is one of us_citizen permanent_resident refugee asylee

    Must be a U.S. citizen or qualified immigrant. California extends benefits to some immigrant categories not covered federally.

    Source
  4. 4. ABAWD Work Requirements
    age is between 18 and 49

    Able-bodied adults without dependents (ABAWDs) aged 18-49 must meet work requirements unless exempt.

    OR satisfy any one of the following exemptions:

    • OR county_abawd_waiver = true - County has ABAWD waiver
    • OR has_disability = true - Has a disability
    • OR has_dependent_child = true - Cares for a dependent child
